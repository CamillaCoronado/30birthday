🍑 Peach Town

Camilla's Discovery Map🌿

may 8th · your 30th birthday · brigham city, utah

oh my goodness, you're finally here! 🎉
it looks like you've been living in this town for a while now,
but there are still so many things left to discover!
today is a very special day. shall we explore together?

🌅
🚗 10 min drive south · willard bay
The Big Peaceful Water

oh, you haven't been to willard bay yet?! it's just a short drive south of town! the water is so calm and clear in the morning, and all sorts of birds come to visit. it's been here your whole time in town and i think it's been waiting for you to come say hello!

✨ task
stand right at the water's edge and say one thing out loud that you're leaving behind. the herons are very discreet, so don't worry!
🔓 unlocks: knowing your bay
🚂
🚶 walkable · forest st · open fri 2–6pm
The Old Train Station

wow, this building has been here since 1907! i've read that back in the old days, the whole town would bring their peaches and cherries here to be loaded onto trains and sent all over the country! can you imagine?! the trains don't stop here anymore, but the building is still just as lovely. i think the history is kind of soaked into the walls!

✨ task
find one thing inside that surprises you! it doesn't have to be a big thing. just something you didn't expect to find here!
🔓 unlocks: your town's railroad era
⏰ opens at 2pm — plan accordingly
🔍
🚶 walkable · main street
The Secret in the Wall

oh, this is one of my favorite things to tell new residents about! there's a plaque on main street marking where the old cooperative store used to be, but if you look very carefully at the brickwork right next to it, you can still see the outline of the original building! it's been gone for over a hundred years but it's still kind of... there! like a little ghost!

✨ task
find the ghost outline in the bricks and take a photo! you'll probably walk right past it the first time. that's okay! most people do!
🔓 unlocks: seeing what others miss
🏛️
🚶 walkable · forest street · free
The Museum With 12,000 Things In It

did you know we have a museum right here on forest street?! and it's completely free! it has fossils and crystals and pioneer artifacts and a room that glows under black light — i'm not totally sure what's in all of it, actually. i think that's kind of the point! it's in an old school building and some of the things inside have just been sitting there quietly for a hundred and fifty years!

✨ task
find the one thing that gives you a feeling you can't quite name. stand in front of it for a whole minute. take a photo. no caption needed!
🔓 unlocks: your weird museum
🦆
🚶 walkable · forest street · rees pioneer park
The Duck Pond

rees pioneer park is one of my very favorite spots in town! there's a little bridge and a fountain and ducks who are very friendly if you approach them calmly. residents have been sitting on these benches since before utah was even a state, which i find very comforting somehow! sometimes the best thing to do in a town is just... sit in it for a little while!

✨ task
sit down on a bench. write three things you actually WANT to do this summer — not things you should do, things you genuinely want! screenshot it! that's your summer bucket list now, it's official!
🔓 unlocks: your summer
⭐
🚶 walkable · main street
The Big Glowing Arch

the gateway arch has three hundred and fifty light bulbs in it! the townspeople paid for it themselves back in 1908 because they wanted main street to glow, which i think is just the loveliest reason to do something. it still lights up! you've probably walked right under it without really looking! today you should look!

✨ task
stand directly underneath it and look up! then get someone to take a photo of you there — with your flowers! this is the birthday photo!
🔓 unlocks: your arch, your town
🎱
🚶 walkable · 21 w forest st
The Tavern — You Made It!!

oh, b&b billiards!! did you know it's been here since 1903?! that's older than the arch and older than the train station! it has seen every single kind of night this town has ever had, and tonight it gets to see yours! you explored your whole town today! you found things most residents never look for! now you get to celebrate with everyone you love!

✨ task
when someone asks how old you are tonight, say "thirty!" without hesitating — just once, nice and proud! then order whatever you like! you've earned it!!
🔓 unlocks: your night
